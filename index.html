About

I'm a fieldbuilding fellow at the Astra Fellowship, working on talent pipelines and hiring needs in AI safety. Previously, I founded and led the EA group at Jagiellonian University in Kraków, which later grew into a city-wide student group. From 2017 to 2023 I volunteered with Anima International, and for two of those years coordinated their Polish campaign against live carp sales.

Outside of work, you'll find me painting, crocheting, bouldering, or dancing.
